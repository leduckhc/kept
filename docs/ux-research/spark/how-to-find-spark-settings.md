**Source:** https://sparkmailapp.com/help/general/how-to-find-spark-settings

[Mac](#) [Windows](#) [Android](#) [iOS](#)

There are several ways to access Spark Settings:

* Shortcut - open **Spark Desktop** and use the **⌘,** shortcut (press and hold **⌘** and press **,** );
* From the sidebar - open **Spark Desktop** and click the **Settings** icon at the bottom left;
* From the Menu Bar - open **Spark Desktop** > Click **Spark** **Desktop** in the Menu Bar at the top > **Settings**.
