# Customize Notifications

**Source:** https://sparkmailapp.com/help/add-manage-accounts/customize-notifications

---

There are four types of notifications in Spark:

**All** - get a notification for every incoming message.

**Smart** - mutes strangers and automated emails.

**Priority** - Spark notifies you only about senders and threads you marked as Priority.

**No Notifications** - you don’t receive any notifications.

Spark allows you to choose different notification types for different email accounts.

## Set notification actions

In Spark for iOS and Android, you can set quick actions for notifications on the lock screen. For example, you can mark an email as read or archive it without unlocking your device.

[iOS](#)[Android](#)

1. Tap the menu icon ![](https://static.helpjuice.com/helpjuice_production/uploads/upload/image/6617/direct/1588250623195-1588250623195.png)  at the top left.
2. Open **Settings > Notifications**.
3. In the **Notification Actions** section, tap **Primary** or **Secondary Action**.
4. Choose one of the actions: Mark as Read, Done, or Delete.

To block Notifications Actions when your device is locked, follow this guide:

1. Tap the menu icon ![](https://static.helpjuice.com/helpjuice_production/uploads/upload/image/6617/direct/1588250623195-1588250623195.png)  at the top left.
2. Open **Settings > Security**.
3. Protect Notification Actions > Enable the toggle. From now on, Notifications Actions will only be available when your device is unlocked.

1. Tap the menu icon ![](https://static.helpjuice.com/helpjuice_production/uploads/upload/image/6617/direct/1588250613212-1588250613212.png)  at the top left.
2. Tap **Settings > Notifications**.
3. In the **Actions** section, tap **Primary** or **Secondary Action**.
4. Choose one of the actions: Mark as Read, Done, or Delete.

To block Notifications Actions when your device is locked, follow this guide:

1. Tap the menu icon ![](https://static.helpjuice.com/helpjuice_production/uploads/upload/image/6617/direct/1588250613212-1588250613212.png)  at the top left.
2. Tap **Settings > Security**.
3. Protect notification actions > Enable the toggle. From now on, Notifications Actions will only be available when your device is unlocked.

## Adjust notification sounds

[Mac](#)[iOS](#)[Android](#)

### To mute sounds in Spark:

1. On your Mac, open **System Settings > Notifications**.
2. Select Spark from the list of apps.
3. Untick **Play sound for notifications**.

1. Tap the menu icon ![](https://static.helpjuice.com/helpjuice_production/uploads/upload/image/6617/direct/1588250599454-1588250599454.png)  at the top left.
2. Open **Settings > Sound Preferences**.
3. Tick either **Spark Sounds or Standard iOS Sounds**.
4. Enable the **Sent mail sound** toggle to play a special sound when you send an email.

### To mute sounds in Spark:

1. Open the **iOS Settings app**.
2. Tap **Notifications** and find Spark on the list of your apps.
3. Disable the **Sounds** toggle.

Spark lets you set different notification sounds for each of your accounts. To use this feature, please make sure you have Spark version 2.3.2 or later.

1. Tap the menu icon ![](https://static.helpjuice.com/helpjuice_production/uploads/upload/image/6617/direct/1588250592702-1588250592702.png)  at the top left.
2. Tap **Settings > Notifications**.
3. Pick the desired email account.
4. In the **Sound Set** section, select either **Spark Sounds** or **System Sounds**.
5. If you’ve selected **System Sounds**, you can choose one of the ringtones by tapping **Sound**.

If you’d like to mute notification sounds, tap **Notification style** and select either **Medium** or **Low**.

You can also mute sounds in Spark in the Notifications section of the Settings app on your device. Check [this article](https://support.google.com/android/answer/9079661?hl=en) for more details.

## Choose the notification style

Spark for Android lets you set the level of importance for your notifications which defines how they should behave. You can select different notification styles for each of your accounts.

[Android](#)

Here are the notification styles available in Spark:

* **Urgent** – notification makes a sound and stays on the home screen until you act on it.
* **High** – makes a sound, is displayed in the status bar, on the lock screen, and in the notification shade.
* **Medium** – no sound, is displayed in the status bar, on the lock screen, and in the notification shade.
* **Low** – no sound, doesn’t appear in the status bar. You can still find it in the notification shade.

How to customize the notification style:

1. Tap the menu icon ![](https://static.helpjuice.com/helpjuice_production/uploads/upload/image/6617/direct/1588250583801-1588250583801.png)  at the top left.
2. Tap **Settings > Notifications**.
3. Pick the desired email account.
4. Tap **Notification style** and select one of the options.

## Turn on LED notifications

Spark for Android lets you customize LED notification colors for each of your accounts.

[Android](#)

1. Tap the menu icon ![](https://static.helpjuice.com/helpjuice_production/uploads/upload/image/6617/direct/1588250566748-1588250566748.png)  at the top left.
2. Go to **Settings > Notifications**.
3. Pick the desired email account.
4. Make sure that the **Notification style** is set to anything but **Low**.
5. Tap **LED** and select one of the options.
