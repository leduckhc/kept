The Gatekeeper feature puts you in control of who’s allowed to email you - and who isn’t. The first time someone emails you, you get to decide if you want to get emails from them again: you can either accept the new sender or decline them. The senders are not notified if you block them in Gatekeeper.

**Note:**Gatekeeper is a part of [Spark paid](https://sparkmailapp.com/plans-comparison) functionality.

## Setting Gatekeeper

Once you accessed the Gatekeeper settings, you can choose where to accept or block senders:

* **Default Setting** - Screen new senders before inbox (Emails from new senders are displayed in the New senders section on the top of Inbox)
* Screen new senders inside inbox (Gatekeeper is displayed on the top of email from a new sender upon opening)
* Don't review (Accept all new senders)

**Tip:**If you would like to bulk accept or bulk block new senders, select the “On top of the Inbox” option.

## Using Gatekeeper

Once the Gatekeeper is on, you will be asked whether to accept or block the sender every time somebody with a new email address emails you. The senders are not notified regardless of your choice. For them, it looks the same as if they have never received a reply from you.

**Note:**The Gatekeeper blocks email addresses but not the domains.

### Bulk accept or bulk block

Besides accepting or blocking senders separately, you can bulk accept or bulk block all the senders with just a few clicks. To do so:

[Mac](#) [Windows](#) [Android](#) [iOS](#)

1. Open Inbox and click **New Senders** above the new sender cards or the … > **Show All** at the top right.
2. At the top right, click the thumb-up to accept all the senders or a thumb-down to block them.

**Note:**This option is available if you choose Gatekeeper to be on top of the Inbox.

### Block domain

[Mac](#) [Windows](#) [Android](#) [iOS](#)

1. Open the needed email.
2. Press **⌘ + K** to open Command Center.
3. Type **Block**.
4. **Block All < domain name >**, or press **⌘ + Option + Shift + B** Shortcut.
5. New emails from selected domain will be Blocked.
