# Enable Notification Badges

Source: https://sparkmailapp.com/help/add-manage-accounts/enable-notification-badges
Scraped: 2026-05-31T10:50:16Z

---

Spark can show you the number of unread emails or all messages in your inbox on the app icon. By default, badges are disabled, but you can turn them on.

[Mac](#) [Windows](#) [Android](#) [iOS](#)

1. [Open Spark Settings](https://sparkmailapp.com/1442243-how-to-find-spark-settings).
2. Select**Notifications** **>** **Badges**.
3. Enable the **App icon badge**.
4. Select one of the options in the **Badge count section**. To see badges only for unread emails, choose **New Messages Only**. To see badges for all emails in your inbox, choose **Whole Inbox**.
5. Enable the email addresses you want to use badges with.

**Note:**To show and update badges, Spark should run in the background. Spark doesn't show badges when you quit it.

To make sure the badge count works correctly on your Mac, follow these steps:

1. In Spark, open [Spark Settings](https://spark3.helpjuice.com/1442243-how-to-find-spark-settings#)**> Accounts > [your account] > Notifications**. Make sure either the **All, Priority** or **Smart** option is ticked.
2. On your Mac, open **System Settings > Notifications > Spark Desktop**. Make sure the **Badge application icon** option is enabled.

1. In Spark, open **Settings > Email Accounts > [your account] > Notifications**. Make sure either **All, Priority** or **Smart** option is enabled.
2. In the iOS Settings app, open **Notifications > Spark**. Make sure the **Allow Notifications** and **Badges** toggles are on.
