# Pins and Priority senders and threads

**Source:** https://sparkmailapp.com/help/sending-emails/pin-and-priority

---

When it comes to an important email you definitely don’t want to lose, or a sender whose emails are your top priority while dealing with Inbox, Spark has it covered. This reading will discuss what the Pin and Priority features are and how to use them.

## Pin

Pinning a message moves it to the Pins section so it's easy to find later. It’s up to you to decide where this section would be: on the sidebar or right in your Inbox (grouped like Newsletters and Notifications). To set pins according to your preferences, do the following:

[Mac](#) [Windows](#) [Android](#) [iOS](#)

1. Open **Spark Desktop** > Select **Settings**.
2. Switch to the**Inbox**section > **Email Groups** > **Pinned**.
3. Select the preferred option.

## Priority setting

Marking important senders or threads as Priority makes new messages from them appear on top of your Inbox. Emails from priority senders and threads marked as a Priority are indicated by this![](https://static.helpjuice.com/helpjuice_production/uploads/upload/image/11007/direct/1656593042777-Priority-Selected.png)symbol and are highlighted with light orange.

### How to mark a thread as a Priority

[Mac](#) [Windows](#) [Android](#) [iOS](#)

Open Spark Desktop and do any of the following:

* hover over the needed email in your Inbox and press **I**.
* hover over the needed email in your Inbox and click the priority![](https://static.helpjuice.com/helpjuice_production/uploads/upload/image/11007/direct/1656593076230-Priority.png)button.
* when the email is opened, press **I** or click the priority![](https://static.helpjuice.com/helpjuice_production/uploads/upload/image/11007/direct/1656593076230-Priority.png)button at the top right.

*To remove a Priority mark*, follow the same steps.

**Tip:**You can also perform these actions using the [Command Center](https://sparkmailapp.com/general/spark-command-center).
