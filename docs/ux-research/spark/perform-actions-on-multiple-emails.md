# Perform Actions on Multiple Emails

Source: https://sparkmailapp.com/help/manage-your-inbox/perform-actions-on-multiple-emails
Scraped: 2026-05-31T10:50:50Z

---

In Spark, you can easily select multiple emails and archive, delete, or pin them all at once.

[Your browser does not support HTML5 video.](https://static.helpjuice.com/helpjuice_production/uploads/upload/image/11007/direct/1746447816778-multiple%20actions%20multi_07.mp4)

[Mac](#) [Windows](#) [Android](#) [iOS](#)

1. Click the space between the sidebar and any email to enter the select mode. Then, select the needed emails. Press **Cmd ⌘ A**to select all emails in the current folder.

![](https://static.helpjuice.com/helpjuice_production/uploads/upload/image/11007/direct/1661936466775-Screenshot%202022-08-31%20at%2011.59.12.png)

2. At the bottom, choose an action you want Spark to perform.

To cancel the selection, press**Esc** or click **Clear Selection ![](https://static.helpjuice.com/helpjuice_production/uploads/upload/image/11007/direct/1661936637131-Cross.png)**at the bottom. To cancel an action, tap**Undo** at the bottom of the screen or press **CTRL Z**in under 5 seconds. It’s also possible to manually cancel an action later.

**Note:**When you delete emails from the Trash folder, they cannot be recovered.

To cancel an action, tap**Undo** at the bottom of the screen or press **(CTRL) Z**in under 5 seconds. It’s also possible to manually cancel an action later.

1. Long tap an email in your inbox.
2. Tap the other emails you want to select. To choose all emails in the current folder, tap **Select all** at the top right.
3. To cancel the selection, tap **Clear selection** at the top right. To deselect a single email, just tap on it.
4. After selecting the desired emails, choose one of the actions on the top toolbar:

![](https://static.helpjuice.com/helpjuice_production/uploads/upload/image/6617/direct/1588251492537-1588251492537.png)  - Bulk Delete

![](https://static.helpjuice.com/helpjuice_production/uploads/upload/image/6617/direct/1588251496283-1588251496283.png)  - Move emails to another folder

![](https://static.helpjuice.com/helpjuice_production/uploads/upload/image/6617/direct/1588251500774-1588251500774.png)  - Batch Snooze

**Note:**The bulk actions toolbar directly correlates with [swipe actions settings](https://sparkmailapp.com/manage-your-inbox/manage-emails-with-swipes): four actions chosen as swipe ones are displayed on the toolbar and all the others are reachable through the **More** icon ![](https://lh4.googleusercontent.com/OmI1quIvvHrVwGuRdFmkowly63cviwQJoDFpXShS_yzMotBQaI_Pj63gwQHvgwlg2XcUYRuMph63P9mWm1xNHD4M95e2s_HDdcb-nLVikvkkBIua94lzBAwzZf7FdABqU6JZnKUm). You can always change the swipe actions via **Settings** > **Personalization** > **Swipes**.

5. To cancel an action, tap **Undo** at the bottom of the screen in under 5 seconds. It’s also possible to manually cancel an action later.

**Note:**When you delete emails from the **Trash** folder, they cannot be recovered.

1. Tap and hold an email in your inbox to trigger the selection mode.
2. In a pop-up menu, tap**Select**.
3. Tap at the other emails you want to select or tap **Select All** at the top right to act on all emails in the current folder.
4. After selecting the desired emails, choose one of the actions at the bottom:
