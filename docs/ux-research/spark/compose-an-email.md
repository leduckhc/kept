Composer tools in Spark help create professional emails and offer smooth email writing and collaboration experience. Composer tools include choosing font, color, and size in your messages, embedding media, editing inline attachments, automatic link recognition and turning them into hyperlinks, and much more.

1. To start composing an email, open Spark and do any of the following:

* click ![Compose.jpg](https://static.helpjuice.com/helpjuice_production/uploads/upload/image/11007/1887201/Compose.jpg)  at the top right;
* press **C;**
* If you want to reply to an incoming email, open it and click **Reply** ![Reply.jpg](https://static.helpjuice.com/helpjuice_production/uploads/upload/image/11007/1887331/Reply.jpg) at the bottom right.

2. Check the email address you are sending an email from. If you want to send an email from a different account, click the current email address and choose a different one.
3. Start typing the recipient’s name or email address in the **To:** field and select the suggestion from Spark. You can enter multiple recipients here. Click **Cc:** or **Bcc:** on the right to send a copy of this email to other people.
4. Fill out the **Subject** line and write your message.
5. Click **Send**

**Note:**For the 5 seconds after sending an email, you can click **UNDO**at the bottom left to undo this action. The email will appear in the Drafts folder.

## Select the font of your emails

Spark allows you to select the font and font size while composing an email.

[Mac](#) [Windows](#) [Android](#) [iOS](#)

In the email composer, type any text > Select the text > Click on the Default font at the Formatting Toolbar.

## Resize inline images
