# Choose the account name, title, and color

**Source:** https://sparkmailapp.com/help/add-manage-accounts/choose-the-account-name-title-and-color

---

[Mac](#) [Windows](#) [Android](#) [iOS](#)

1. [Open Spark Settings](https://sparkmailapp.com/help/general/how-to-find-spark-settings).
2. Click**Accounts** and select your account.
3. Type your name in the **Name:** field. The name is displayed in the To: or From: field when you send or receive an email. If your account has no name, these fields are filled with your email address.
4. Fill out the **Title:** field. The title is used only in Spark. This is the name of your account shown on the left sidebar. You can enter any title you wish to navigate your inbox easily.
5. Return to **Accounts** and click on the **Color** **coding for accounts** to choose the color for your account. For example, you can pick red for your work account and use green for personal emails. It allows you to see which account each email in your inbox belongs to.

**Note:**To enable color coding for accounts, open **Settings** >**Appearance** and make sure the **Color coding for accounts** option is enabled.
