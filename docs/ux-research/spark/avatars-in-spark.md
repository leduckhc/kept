## Where do avatars come from? How can I change them?

Spark pulls avatars from multiple sources, and at the moment there’s no option to change avatars right in the app.

### 1. **Teams**

Since you are a team member, it is possible to set your own avatar no matter what [role](https://sparkmailapp.com/spark-for-teams/team-roles) you have. All your team members with avatars enabled will see your photo both in the team and in personal conversations.

### 2. **Gravatar**

Spark can also pull avatars from your [Gravatar](https://en.gravatar.com/) profile. If you are not a team member nor a contact in someone’s address book, Spark will search for your avatar on Gravatar.

### 3. **Clearbit**

[Clearbit](https://clearbit.com) is used by Spark to pull avatars of different services such as Trello, Jira, Google, etc.

**Tip:**Spark Desktop on Mac and Spark on iOS may also sync avatars from your Contacts app if access is granted.
