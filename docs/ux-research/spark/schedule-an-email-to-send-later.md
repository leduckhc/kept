# Schedule an email to send later

**Source:** https://sparkmailapp.com/help/sending-emails/schedule-an-email-to-send-later

---

In Spark, you can set an email to be sent later. This feature is helpful when you want to make sure other people receive your email when they are most likely to read it.

[Your browser does not support HTML5 video.](https://static.helpjuice.com/helpjuice_production/uploads/upload/image/11007/direct/1746448291161-send%20later%20Mac_later_07.mp4)

The scheduled emails are stored on our server in the encrypted form. They sync across your devices, and the email will be sent even if Spark is closed.

**Note:**The Send Later feature won’t work if you remove Spark from all your devices.

## Schedule an email

[Mac](#) [Windows](#) [Android](#) [iOS](#)

1. Start [writing or replying to an email](https://sparkmailapp.com/sending-emails/compose-an-email).
2. Click ![](https://static.helpjuice.com/helpjuice_production/uploads/upload/image/11007/direct/1659440046945-Schedule.png)  at the top right.
3. Type the time for this email or select it from the list. You can schedule emails to be sent up to 6 months ahead.
4. Click the**Send**button at the top right. The email will be kept in the Outbox folder until the Send Later time is due.

2. Here, you can see your scheduled emails. Click an email to see its details.
3. Click the sending time at the top to change it.

## Customize the Send Later time

[Mac](#) [Windows](#) [Android](#) [iOS](#)

1. [Open Spark Settings](https://sparkmailapp.com/general/how-to-find-spark-settings).
2. Select **Scheduling > Send Later**.
3. Toggle on the options you wish to see while scheduling an email.
4. Select the time for the needed options.

1. [Open Spark Settings](https://sparkmailapp.com/general/how-to-find-spark-settings).
2. Select **Scheduling > Send Later**.
3. Toggle on the options you wish to see while scheduling an email.
4. Select the time for the needed options.

1. Tap the menu icon ![](https://static.helpjuice.com/helpjuice_production/uploads/upload/image/6617/direct/1588249742905-1588249742905.png)  at the top left.
2. Tap **Settings** ![](https://static.helpjuice.com/helpjuice_production/uploads/upload/image/6617/direct/1588249751259-1588249751259.png)  at the bottom.
3. Select **Scheduling > Send Later**.
4. Tick the options you wish to see while scheduling an email.
5. Tap an option (e.g. Tomorrow) to customize the time.

Alternatively, you can customize the Send later time right in the email composer:

1. While writing an email, tap ![](https://static.helpjuice.com/helpjuice_production/uploads/upload/image/6617/direct/1588249759919-1588249759919.png)  at the bottom.
2. Tap ![](https://static.helpjuice.com/helpjuice_production/uploads/upload/image/6617/direct/1588249770531-1588249770531.png)  and select the needed options.

1. Tap the menu icon ![](https://static.helpjuice.com/helpjuice_production/uploads/upload/image/6617/direct/1588249734140-1588249734140.png)  at the top left.
2. Choose **Settings > Scheduling > Send Later**.
3. Tick the options you wish to see while scheduling an email.
4. Tap an option (e.g. Tomorrow) to customize the time.
