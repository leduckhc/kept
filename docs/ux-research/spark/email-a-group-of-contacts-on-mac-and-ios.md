# Email a group of contacts on Mac and iOS

**Source:** https://sparkmailapp.com/help/sending-emails/email-a-group-of-contacts-on-mac

---

Spark for iOS and Mac supports contact lists created in the Contacts app on your Mac or on the iCloud webpage. Lists such as “Family” or “Football team” are a great way to organize your contacts and email them all at once.

Start typing a list name in the “To:” field, select a list, and Spark adds the email addresses of all list members.

To use list of contacts in Spark, please make sure the app has access to your contacts:

* On your **Mac**, open **System Settings > Privacy & Security > Contacts >** Tick **Spark**.
* On your **iPhone or iPad**, open **Settings > Spark >** Enable the **Contacts** toggle.

## Create a list of contacts

There are two ways to create a list of contacts you can use in Spark:

In the Contacts app on your Mac:

1. Launch the **Contacts** app.
2. Click **File** at the top left.
3. Select **New List**.
4. Type the list name.
5. Click on the plus sign at the bottom to add people.

Please check [the instructions from Apple](https://support.apple.com/en-ca/guide/contacts/create-groups-of-contacts-adrb3280fe91/12.0/mac/10.14) for more details.

**Note:**To access a contact list on all your Apple devices, please add this list to the **iCloud**section on the sidebar (not **On My Mac)**.

On the iCloud webpage:

1. Sign into [iCloud.com](https://www.icloud.com/#contacts) with your Apple ID.
2. Click on the plus next to**Lists**.
3. Select **New List**.
4. To add people, select the plus sign on the top right and choose New Contact. You can also click All Contacts on the sidebar and drag & drop the needed contacts to your list.

**Note:**Smart Lists are currently not supported in Spark.

Check [the article from Apple](https://support.apple.com/kb/ph2667?locale=en_US) for more details.

After creating a new list, restart Spark to update your contacts.
