# Add More Email Accounts to Spark

**Source:** https://sparkmailapp.com/help/add-manage-accounts/add-more-email-accounts-to-spark

---

[Mac](#) [Windows](#) [Android](#) [iOS](#)

1. [Open Spark Settings](https://sparkmailapp.com/general/how-to-find-spark-settings).
2. Select **Accounts >** **Add an Account**.
3. Follow the onscreen instructions to sign in. If you need additional settings, click **Additional Settings**. Here, you can choose ports, email server, and the type of protection.
4. Your email provider will ask if you allow Spark to access your account. Click **Allow** or **Agree**.

## Select the default account

When you use multiple accounts, you can choose a default one. When you compose an email, the From: field will contain your default email address.

[Mac](#) [Windows](#) [Android](#) [iOS](#)

1. [Open Spark Settings](https://sparkmailapp.com/help/general/how-to-find-spark-settings).
2. Click **Composer** **>****Send emails by default from**.
3. Tick one of your accounts from the list or choose the **Last Used** option. In this case, the last account you used to email someone is the default.
