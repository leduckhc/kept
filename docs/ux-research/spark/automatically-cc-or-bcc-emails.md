# Automatically Cc or Bcc emails

**Source:** https://sparkmailapp.com/help/sending-emails/automatically-cc-or-bcc-emails

---

When you send emails in Spark, you can automatically Cc or Bcc other people. This feature is handy when you want to keep your teammates in the loop while communicating with your partners or clients.

## Set up auto Cc/Bcc

[Mac](#) [Windows](#) [Android](#) [iOS](#)

1. [Open Spark Settings](https://sparkmailapp.com/general/how-to-find-spark-settings).
2. Select **Composer >****Auto BCC**.
3. Enable the toggle next to the needed account.
4. Select **Cc:** or **Bcc:**.
5. Enter the email address you want to Cc/Bcc.

Now, when you compose an email from the selected account, Spark automatically fills the Cc: or Bcc: field with the email you’ve specified.

[Mac](#) [Windows](#) [Android](#) [iOS](#)

1. [Open Spark Settings](https://sparkmailapp.com/general/how-to-find-spark-settings).
2. Select **Composer >****Auto BCC**.
3. Disable the toggle next to the needed account.
