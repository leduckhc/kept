# Remove an email account from Spark

Source: https://sparkmailapp.com/help/add-manage-accounts/remove-an-email-account-from-spark
Scraped: 2026-05-31T10:50:13Z

---

Removing an account from Spark won’t delete your emails, as they are saved on the email server. You can always [add your account again](https://sparkmailapp.com/add-manage-accounts/add-more-email-accounts-to-spark) and continue working with your emails.

**Note:**You can add an unlimited number of email accounts to Spark and remove the ones you don’t need. However, adding too many accounts may affect Spark performance.

[Mac](#) [Windows](#) [Android](#) [iOS](#)

1. [Open Spark Settings](https://sparkmailapp.com/general/how-to-find-spark-settings).
2. Select **Accounts** and click the account you want to remove.
3. Scroll down and click**Remove Account**.
4. In the pop-up message, click **Remove**.

1. [Open Spark Settings](https://sparkmailapp.com/general/how-to-find-spark-settings).
2. Select **Accounts** and click the account you want to remove.
3. Scroll down and click**Remove Account**.
4. In the pop-up message, click **Remove**.

1. Tap the menu icon ![](https://static.helpjuice.com/helpjuice_production/uploads/upload/image/6617/direct/1588258987005-1588258987005.png)  at the top left.
2. Open **Settings** **>****Mail Accounts**.
3. Choose the account you want to delete, scroll to the bottom and tap **Remove Account**.
4. In the pop-up message, tap **Delete**.

2. Open **Settings > Mail Accounts**.

If you want to delete data about your accounts and preferences from Spark Desktop, open the**Settings**section at the bottom of the sidebar>**Remove your data.**

**Note:**If you try to delete an email account used as your Email for Sync, you'll receive a warning from Spark. When you remove your Email for Sync, you'll also be logged out from the other email accounts you added to Spark. Learn more about the [sync option in Spark](https://sparkmailapp.com/sync-spark-between-devices/sync-spark-across-multiple-devices).
