# Set up an email alias

**Source:** https://sparkmailapp.com/help/sending-emails/set-up-an-email-alias

---

In Spark, you can use an alias to conceal your email address from the recipient. For example, aliases might come in handy when a person has to use their account for business purposes, but the name of the account is not appropriate for professional situations.

Spark supports aliases for Gmail, iCloud, Outlook, Microsoft 365, and Yahoo email accounts. You can add as many aliases as you like.

**Note:**Google aliases will be imported automatically upon [adding a Google account](https://sparkmailapp.com/help/add-manage-accounts/connect-to-your-email-account-in-spark) to Spark.

To learn more about aliases for Shared Inboxes, please check [this article](https://sparkmailapp.com/help/spark-for-teams/aliases-for-shared-inboxes).

**Tip:**Spark also supports custom iCloud domains as aliases to your Primary iCloud account.

Before adding an alias to Spark, please set it up in the settings of your email provider. Follow one of these instructions to create an alias:

* [Gmail](https://support.google.com/mail/answer/22370?hl=en)
* [iCloud](https://support.apple.com/kb/ph2622?locale=en_US)
* [Outlook](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/office/add-or-remove-an-email-alias-in-outlook-com-459b1989-356d-40fa-a689-8f285b13f1f2)
* [Microsoft 365](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-365/admin/email/add-another-email-alias-for-a-user?view=o365-worldwide)

## Add an alias to Spark

[Mac](#) [Windows](#) [Android](#) [iOS](#)

1. [Open Spark Settings](https://sparkmailapp.com/general/how-to-find-spark-settings).
2. Select **Accounts** and click the needed account.
3. Click **Aliases**and enter your alias name and email address. They should be the same as in the settings of your email provider.
4. Click the **Create** button.
5. For Google accounts, press **Sync from Server** to sync all added aliases automatically.

While composing an email, click your email address at the top right and select your alias to use with this email.

To make an alias your default email address, open **Spark Settings**> Composer and select the alias in the **Send emails by d****efault from**section.

**Note:**The default email address appears in the **From:** field only when you compose a new email. It isn't applied automatically when you reply to an email in an existing thread.

## Delete an alias

[Mac](#) [Windows](#) [Android](#) [iOS](#)

1. [Open Spark Settings](https://sparkmailapp.com/general/how-to-find-spark-settings).
2. Select **Accounts** and click the needed account.
3. Click **Aliases**section, select the alias you want to delete.
4. Click the **Delete** button.
