# How to check your Spark version

Source: https://sparkmailapp.com/help/general/how-to-check-what-spark-version-you-use
Scraped: 2026-05-31T10:50:06Z

---

Please follow these steps to know the current Spark version installed on your device.

[Mac](#) [Windows](#) [Android](#) [iOS](#)

1. Launch Spark > Click Spark Desktop in the top left corner of the screen.
2. Click About Spark Desktop > The current version will be displayed here.

![](https://static.helpjuice.com/helpjuice_production/uploads/upload/image/11007/direct/1751475894536-Screenshot%202025-07-02%20at%206.58.08%E2%80%AFPM.png)

1. Launch Spark > Click the downward-pointing arrow in the top right corner of the Spark window.
2. Click About Spark Desktop > The current version will be displayed here.

![](https://static.helpjuice.com/helpjuice_production/uploads/upload/image/11007/direct/1751476054121-image%20(21).png)

**OR**

1. Open Windows Settings > Apps.
2. Apps & features > Spark > Advanced Options.
3. Check the app version number under the Specifications section.

1. Tap the menu icon ![](https://static.helpjuice.com/helpjuice_production/uploads/upload/image/6617/direct/1588256700218-1588256700218.png)  at the top left.
2. Open **Settings > Support >****Legal Notes**.
3. The current version will be displayed here.
