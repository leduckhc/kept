# How to download Spark

**Source:** https://sparkmailapp.com/help/general/how-to-download-spark

---

The Spark email client is available on multiple platforms and is free to download. While it serves as an excellent email client for free, there also are premium features for teams and individuals available under subscription. Continue reading to learn how to download Spark on your device.

[Mac](#) [Windows](#) [Android](#) [iOS](#)

Spark on Mac website version requires macOS 11.6 or later. Spark on Mac App Store version requires macOS 12.0.0 or later. [How to find out which macOS your Mac is using.](https://support.apple.com/en-in/HT201260)

[Link to download Spark](https://sparkmailapp.com/download)  
  
[Link to download Spark on the App Store](https://apps.apple.com/ca/app/spark-mail-ai-email-inbox/id6445813049?mt=12)

[Link to Release Notes for Spark Desktop on Mac](https://sparkmailapp.com/spark3/mac/changelog)

Spark on Windows requires 64-bit Windows version 10 or later.

[Link to download Spark](https://apps.microsoft.com/store/detail/XPFCS9QJBKTHVZ)

[Link to Release Notes for Spark Desktop on Windows](https://sparkmailapp.com/spark3/win/changelog)

[Link to download Spark for Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.readdle.spark&hl=uk&gl=US)

[Link to download Spark for Huawei](https://appgallery.huawei.com/app/C103789413)

Spark on **iPhone**, **iPad**, and **iPod touch** requires iOS 16.0 or later, on **Apple Watch** - watchOS 7.0 or later. [How to find the software version on your device.](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT201685)

[Link to download Spark](https://apps.apple.com/ua/app/spark-email-app-by-readdle/id997102246)
