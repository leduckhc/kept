# Display the Inbox of each account separately

Source: https://sparkmailapp.com/help/manage-your-inbox/display-the-inbox-of-each-account-separately
Scraped: 2026-05-31T10:50:47Z

---

To reveal the list of accounts connected to Spark and work with them separately, follow the steps below:

[Mac](#) [Windows](#) [Android](#) [iOS](#)

1. Open Spark and click![](https://static.helpjuice.com/helpjuice_production/uploads/upload/image/11007/direct/1657307596306-Sidebar.png)at the top left to open the sidebar.
2. Click the small arrow **v** on the left side of the INBOX section. Click the needed account under 'INBOX' to see Inbox only of this account.

![](https://static.helpjuice.com/helpjuice_production/uploads/upload/image/11007/direct/1744205534158-Screenshot%202025-04-09%20at%203.30.22%E2%80%AFPM.png)

1. Open Spark and click![](https://static.helpjuice.com/helpjuice_production/uploads/upload/image/11007/direct/1657307596306-Sidebar.png)at the top left to open the sidebar.
2. Click the small arrow **v** on the left side of the INBOX section. Click the needed account under 'INBOX' to see Inbox only of this account.

To reveal the list of accounts connected to Spark and work with them separately, tap the grey arrow on the right side of the INBOX on the sidebar. Tap an individual account under 'Inbox' to see Inbox only of this account.![](https://static.helpjuice.com/helpjuice_production/uploads/upload/image/11007/direct/1725554167258-Inbox%20A.png)

To see all accounts individually, tap the grey arrow on the INBOX left side on the sidebar. Tap the needed account under 'Inbox' to see Inbox only of this account.![](https://static.helpjuice.com/helpjuice_production/uploads/upload/image/11007/direct/1773770880996-Screenshot%202026-03-17%20at%207.06.33%E2%80%AFPM.png)
